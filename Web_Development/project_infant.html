INFANT PROGRAM

Infants from six weeks to 18 months of age are involved, in a variety of daily activities both indoors and outdoors. Children have the opportunity to be part of group activities such as music and sensory, with a focus on individualized schedules and care.

A healthy and nutritious breakfast, lunch and afternoon snack are prepared daily and served on the premises. Parents are welcome to send formula and/or breast milk in labelled baby bottles. Our staff will also provide baby food and whole milk when infants transition from formula or breast milk.
