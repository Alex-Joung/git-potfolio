PRESCHOOL PROGRAM

Children from two-and-a-half (2.5) to five years of age are involved in a variety of daily activities including art, small and large group activities, outdoor play, music and songs, and computer activities.

A healthy and nutritious breakfast, lunch and afternoon snack are prepared daily and served on the premises. Cots and bedding are provided every afternoon for a rest period. Special bedding, blankets and stuffed animals from home are welcomed and properly stored.
